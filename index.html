Spinnach

Spinnach is a way of loading images nicely by fading them in after showing a loading spinner. Written in only 1.5KB of Javascript and CSS with no dependencies on jQuery or other libraries.

Instructions

Simply load the Spinnach files in the head of your document, preferrably right after you load your site's CSS files:

Wrap your image as follows, add the onload attribute and you're good to go. The default approach assumes you have fixed height and width set on your image wrapper.

Advanced functionality

Feel free to configure Spinnach to your needs. You can configure the size, color and animation timings of your spinner to match your site's color pallette. These configurable variables can be found in src/spinnach.coffee.
